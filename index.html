MUSIC THAT CREATES STORIES

Music for me is about gathering moods and feelings to create something new and different, a sound you’ve only heard in your mind. I get my inspiration and feelings from looking at pictures, to me it’s the best way to enter another world and feel free to create what I'm all about during that moment. Those feelings comes to life via music, through a rare and unique story, and that story is there for you to imagine.

 

My music has over 100 million streams on youtube, and is being used by e.g. Peter McKinnon, "iJustine", Jon Olsson, Matti Haapoja, Nicole Eddy and "Life of Boris". It has also been used in a lot of commercials, TV-shows and series. I'm currently writing and producing music for Epidemic Sound, so most of the music I've produced is signed, curated and published by them. Worth noting is that I produce music under names as "Ooyy", "Luwaks", "Yomoti", "Harvio" and my own name Henrik Olsson.

 

I started my music career as a DJ and electronic music producer around 2010. In the beginning I was more in to mixing songs together on a turn-table but as the time passed, I started discovering the art of writing and producing music and got a bachelors degree in Music Production & Sound Engineering. During my time studying I grew as a music producer and discovered a lot of new music & genres that helped create my unique sound.

 

After three years of music-studies I came in touch with Epidemic Sound and they really liked my music and my different style of producing. So I started working with Epidemic and thats where I am as of today.

OOYY

The music released under the name"Ooyy" has a modern and futuristic sound with a touch of ambient feels. Creating an "Ooyy" track is a fun experience and when you hear the rhythms and sounds of the productions you know it's an "Ooyy" track.

YOMOTI

The magic of Lush Lo-Fi beats with this jazz/soul touches. I must say it's pretty relaxing music to listen to when you're in the mood for it. Music released under "Yomoti" is both fun and uplifting, the crushed beats and jazzy chords with the fun melodies will totally get you set for a great mood.

LUWAKS

My more chill and ambient sounds is released under "Luwaks". The creating process with "Luwaks" is a lot of sound design and getting the right feel to the tracks. I want to create this feeling where you can set your mind free and imagine the track's meaning with your thoughts and feelings.

HARVIO

It's the old but good deep house music that probably fits every lounge in the world...! Harvio is all about the lounge vibes with a summer evening sunset on the beach.
